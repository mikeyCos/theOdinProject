Ingredients

Meat

  • 2 Pounds of chicken, cut into 1 inch square bites; thighs preferably
  • 5-6 Slices of thick cut bacon, cut into 1 inch pieces

Sauce

  • 1 1/5 Cups barbecue sauce
  • 1/3 Cup bourbon
  • 3 Tablespoons dark brown sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons sriracha
  • 1 1/5 Tablespoons yellow mustard
  • 1 Tablespoon neutral oil
  • 1/2 Tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 Teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 1 Teaspoon fresh ground pepper

Steps

  1. Thread alternating pieces of chicken and bacon on to skewers.
  2. Combine all suace ingredients in a large bowl and mix well.
  3. Pre-heat grill to medium high; leave it for about 10 minutes.
  4. Spray grill with non-stick spray, lightly brush both sides of skewers with oil and place on grill.
  5. Cook on first side for 2-3 minutes or until good marks start to appear, then flip.
  6. Now you can start basting the top, cooked side.
  7. Continue grilling, saucing, turning, saucing, turning until gorgeous and done.
