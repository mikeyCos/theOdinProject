Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup cream cheese (softened)
  • 1/2 Cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 12 Slices of bacon (crumbled)

Steps

  1. Fry bacon in pan until crispy, and place on a tray lined with paper towles.
  2. Wash jalapeno peppers, dry, cut in half, and remove seeds. Optional: mix seeds with cream cheese mixture.
  3. Preheat oven to 400F.
  4. In a bowl, mix the cream cheese and shredded chedddar cheese together. Stuff the jalapeno peppers with the cream cheese mixture and sprinkle the bacon on top of the mixture.
  5. Bake in oven for 15-20 minutes.
